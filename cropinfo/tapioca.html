TAPIOCA(Manihot esculenta)


              

In Tamil, the roots of tapioca are called maravalli kizhangu, and are used to prepare chips. Tapioca pearls are referred to as "javvarisi" in Tamil. ... Tapioca is cultivated more in several districts, providing steady income to farmers.Although tapioca is a staple food for millions of people in tropical countries, it provides only carbohydrate food value, and is low in protein, vitamins and minerals. In other countries, it is used as a thickening agent in various manufactured foods.
