SUGARCANE(Saccharum officinarum)


              

Sugarcane, or sugar cane, are several species of tall perennial true grasses of the genus Saccharum, tribe Andropogoneae, native to the warm temperate to tropical regions of South and Southeast Asia, Polynesia and Melanesia, and used for sugar production. Did you know: Sugarcane is the ninth-most valuable crop / livestock product by value ($56,903,836 thousand us dollars)
